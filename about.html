About Our Academy

Zambia's Leading Heavy Equipment & Safety Training Provider

Since 2013, Nirvana Business Solutions has been transforming Zambia's workforce through specialized vocational training. As a Mufulira-based training academy, we deliver certified programs in heavy machinery operation and occupational safety that meet international standards. Our hands-on approach combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills development, preparing operators and safety professionals for real-world challenges in mining, construction, and industrial sectors. With a focus on quality and safety compliance, we've trained thousands of professionals who now contribute to Zambia's growing infrastructure and industrial development.
